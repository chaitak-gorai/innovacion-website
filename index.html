About the Event

Institute of Engineering & Management, Kolkata in association with University of Engineering & Management is organising its annual Techno-Management fest-"Innovación 2020" in April, 2020 . Not only students from all over India and several parts of the world showcase their talent and technical skills here, but Innovación also witnesses lectures of great scholars from reputed educational firms and industries. Innovación features several Innovative Models, grandiose Robotics Competitions, Coding events, Guest lectures, Workshops and many more. The steadfast hard work of a group of Students, Professors, Scholars and Sponsors has helped Innovación emerge as one of the largest Techno- Management Fest in Kolkata. Innovación is one of the biggest and the greatest tech fest in Kolkata. Innovación is an arena to exhibit the creativity and latent talent of students in varied dimensions of technology and management. Innovación caters to a wide variety of disciplines ranging from Robotics, Electronics, Coding and core fundamentals of Science and Mathematics. It is our endeavor to challenge participants to think, ideate, innovate and push themselves to go that extra mile. Here we are not only hosting events from different domains but we would also be having esteemed delegates to glorify the podium of the festival. With various new events on the list we are reaching out for students across the nation from top graduate schools and colleges. We, here at Innovación also intend to educate the interested and for that the festival is home to workshops that shall bring students face to face with the leading and emerging technologies of the world and make them technically adept. The entertainment and gaming industry has always embraced latest technology, and to highlight the very same, Innovación is proud to present an International Robotics Workshop where exhibitors from all over the world shall enthrall students and audiences alike with their mind-blowing displays.
